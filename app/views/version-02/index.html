Apply for a National Insurance number

You only need to apply for a National Insurance number if you work, or plan to work, in the UK.

How to apply

To use this service you must:

  • be in the UK
  • have the right to work in the UK

You will need to :

  1. Apply online.
  2. Book an appointment to show us your identity documents (we will tell you which identity documents to bring with you later in your application).
  3. Attend the appointment.

You will recieve a decision on your application within 8 weeks.
