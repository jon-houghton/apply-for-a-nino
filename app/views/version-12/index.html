Apply for a National Insurance number

You only need to apply for a National Insurance number if you work, or plan to work, in the UK.

A National Insurance number is used to track your tax contributions and benefit payments. It is not a form of identity. Find out more about National Insurance

To apply online you will need to:

  • be in the UK
  • have the right to work in the UK
  • have a passport
  • have a biometric residence permit (BRP)
